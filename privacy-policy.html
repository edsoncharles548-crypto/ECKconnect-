🏠 ECKconnect — Privacy Policy / Sera ya Faragha

Last updated / Ilisasishwa: July 2026
English: This Privacy Policy explains how ECKconnect ("we", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

Kiswahili: Sera hii ya faragha inaeleza jinsi ECKconnect inavyokusanya, kutumia, na kulinda taarifa zako unapotumia app yetu.

1. Information We Collect / Taarifa Tunazokusanya

When you register or use ECKconnect, we may collect: your name, email address, phone number, profile photo, property listings you post (including photos, price, and location), and messages you send within the chat feature.

Kiswahili: Tunaweza kukusanya jina lako, barua pepe, namba ya simu, picha ya profaili, matangazo ya nyumba unayoweka (ikiwemo picha, bei, na mahali), na ujumbe unaotuma kwenye mazungumzo ndani ya app.

2. How We Use Your Information / Jinsi Tunavyotumia Taarifa

We use your information to: create and manage your account, display your property listings to other users, enable communication between property seekers and owners, and improve our services.

Kiswahili: Tunatumia taarifa zako kuunda na kusimamia akaunti yako, kuonyesha matangazo yako kwa watumiaji wengine, kuwezesha mawasiliano kati ya watafuta nyumba na wamiliki, na kuboresha huduma zetu.

3. Data Storage / Uhifadhi wa Taarifa

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services (Firebase Authentication and Firestore Database). We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Kiswahili: Taarifa zako zinahifadhiwa kwa usalama kupitia huduma za Google Firebase. Hatuuzi taarifa zako binafsi kwa watu wengine.

4. Sharing of Information / Ushirikishaji wa Taarifa

Your name, profile photo, and listing details are visible to other users of the app as part of the core functionality (connecting property seekers with owners). Your email and phone number are not publicly displayed unless you choose to share them.

Kiswahili: Jina lako, picha ya profaili, na taarifa za tangazo lako zinaonekana kwa watumiaji wengine wa app kama sehemu ya lengo kuu la app (kuunganisha watafuta nyumba na wamiliki). Barua pepe na namba yako ya simu haziwekwi hadharani isipokuwa uamue kuzishirikisha mwenyewe.

5. Your Rights / Haki Zako

You can edit or delete your profile information and property listings at any time within the app. You may also request account deletion by contacting us.

Kiswahili: Unaweza kuhariri au kufuta taarifa zako za profaili na matangazo yako wakati wowote ndani ya app. Unaweza pia kuomba kufutwa kwa akaunti yako kwa kuwasiliana nasi.

6. Children's Privacy / Faragha ya Watoto

ECKconnect is not intended for use by children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Kiswahili: ECKconnect si kwa ajili ya watumiaji walio chini ya miaka 18. Hatukusanyi kwa makusudi taarifa za watoto.

Kiswahili: Kama una maswali kuhusu sera hii ya faragha, wasiliana nasi kupitia WhatsApp: +255 684 253 244 au barua pepe: edsoncharles548@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy / Mabadiliko ya Sera Hii
